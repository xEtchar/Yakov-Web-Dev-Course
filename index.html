The Choice

Chicken Tikka Masala
Chicken tikka masala is a dish of roasted chunks of chicken tikka in a spicy sauce. The sauce is usually creamy, spiced and orange in colour.
Lamb Vindaloo
Lamb Vindaloo is an Indian curry dish popular in the region of Goa, the surrounding Konkan, and many other parts of India. It is often very spicy, yet tasty.
Veg Biryani
Veg Biryani is a mixed rice dish from the Indian subcontinent. It is popular throughout the subcontinent and among the diaspora from the region.
